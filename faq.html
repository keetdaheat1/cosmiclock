FAQ

What is the star stopwatch?

The star stopwatch is a gentle reminder to be aware of your own horoscope and be thankful of the world, galaxy and universe and for those who give thanks to God.

What is the water container timer for?

The water container timer is to remind yourself that water is key and main thing to sustain life for one another. As human beings we are mostly water, and we share this planet with life thanks to H2O.

What does the pie timer mean?

The pie timer is a subtle hint to check yourself that we are all part of a never-ending carousel and Pi is the key as it relates to cyclical geometry and the polyhedral nature of the physical world.
