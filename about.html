About Cosmiclock

This is a solopreneur developer project to help people wake up after having sleeping troubles, incorporating solfeggio tones, activating chakras, and helping people with insomnia wake up gently, plus supporting others with cortisol and theta-waves in the morning.

Cosmiclock is designed to feel warm, calm, and simple: set an alarm, play a YouTube wake-up track, and use the timers/stopwatch with soothing cosmic visuals.
